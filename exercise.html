Daily 30-Minute Exercise

Relieve your stress through movement and joy

Choose Your Favorite Exercise

Walking

Take a brisk walk around your neighborhood or a nearby park. Walking improves mood, clears the mind, and keeps your heart healthy.

Dancing

Put on your favorite music and dance like nobody's watching. It's fun, expressive, and releases endorphins.

Sports

Join a group to play basketball, football, or any sport you enjoy. Team sports help you bond socially and physically de-stress.

Tip: Stay consistent. Even light exercise every day can drastically reduce anxiety and improve sleep quality.
