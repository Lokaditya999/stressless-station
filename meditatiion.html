Meditation for Stress Relief

Learn simple techniques to calm your mind and reduce stress

Why Meditation Helps

Meditation activates the body's relaxation response, helping to reduce anxiety, lower blood pressure, and enhance emotional well-being. With consistent practice, it strengthens your ability to stay calm under pressure.

Basic Steps to Start

  1. Find a quiet space and sit comfortably.
  2. Close your eyes and take slow, deep breaths.
  3. Focus on your breath, letting thoughts pass without judgment.
  4. Start with 5 minutes daily, gradually increasing time.

Tips for Consistency

  • Set a daily reminder or routine time.
  • Create a peaceful meditation corner at home.
  • Use meditation apps like Headspace or Calm for support.
